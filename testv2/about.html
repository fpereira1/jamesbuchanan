My Story

I enjoy creating beautiful web applications that not only look good but are usable. When I am not designing for the web you'll find me outdoors skiing, kitesurfing, or just basking in the sun.

I have a degree in Industrial Design and a Diploma in Web Development. After starting a web design company in early 2008, I gained a broad range of experience in building client relationships and designing and developing solutions for their needs. Since then I have been an Interaction Designer, UI Developer, UX Designer & UX Consultant see my Linkedin for more details.

I love meeting new people, feel free to contact me.
